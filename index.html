35th Street Bistro is a delightful country European restaurant located in the heart of Seattle's Fremont neighborhood, featuring tastefully prepared food from locally and sustainably grown ingredients, an extensive list of hand-picked wines, and a full bar.

We're open for Dinner every evening from 5pm, serving lunch from 11am Tuesday through Sunday. Brunch is served on Saturday
and Sunday.
