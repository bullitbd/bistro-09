GROWER CHAMPAGNES
There's an entire category of Champagne unknown to most lovers of bubbly that offers diverse styles, the taste of terroir, artisanal winemaking and, most significantly, excellent value. Grower Champagnes are, as the name implies, Champagnes made and sold by smaller grower/producers (or récoltant) from their own grapes. Grower Champagnes typically show terroir more than those from the "grandes marques" from major houses like Moët et Chandon (who produces over 2 million cases annually), Roederer, Veuve Clicot and Roederer - who produce a lot of wine with grapes sourced from independent growers. They also show more of the character of the vintner himself, as these wines are most often handcrafted product made with his personal care. They offer a broad palette of flavors and styles, often far more distinct than the large houses.
We invite you to choose from our special offering of Grower Champagnes, on our List at wonderfully reasonable prices - all to entice you to enjoy the marvel of these wines, and how well they pair with our food!
